Powell Smash

Powell Smash is a local organization of SSBM players north of Columbus in Central Ohio.

We run free-to-enter tournaments pretty much every weekend at someone's house, and attend public tournaments, as well as hosting two to three major, public tournaments with a buy-in a year. We are looking to grow, and if you are nearby Powell or Lewis Center, Ohio, send an email to powellsmash@gmail.com to get involved!

This page is mostly for those players already involved, but if you have an interest in joining us and were given the link by one of our players, welcome! Take a look around and see if you'd want to join us!

Some of our most notable acheievements include one of our players making it out of pools at Shuffle VII, one of our players making fifth in his pool at Genesis 3, one of our players getting 104% on M2K in PM, and three players making it out of pools at Screw It Just Upsmash!

One of main features of this site are our Power Rankings, check those out with the rankings tab above!

More is coming to this site soon, keep your eyes open!
